# Manual Map Reveal Method

Owner action required: add the real full map image as `fallback/map-full.png` and either:

- add one image per reveal step under `fallback/fragments/`, named `fragment-01.png` through `fragment-21.png`, or
- keep the full map open locally and reveal it manually in 21 linear steps.

Fallback reveal rule:

- approval 1 reveals fragment 1
- approval 2 reveals fragment 2
- continue until approval 21 reveals the full map

Quest order does not affect reveal order.
